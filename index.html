Blackjack Game

The goal of blackjack is to build a hand value as close to 21 as possible without going over. The game starts with the dealer dealing out two cards face up to the player and two cards to themselves - one face up and one face down. The card values are tallied based on their numerical rank, with face cards counting as 10 and aces counting as 1 or 11 - whichever benefits the hand the most. The player takes the first turn and can choose to "hit" and be dealt another card from the deck in an attempt to get closer to 21, or "stand" if they are satisfied with their current hand value. If the player goes over 21, they "bust" and automatically lose the hand.
